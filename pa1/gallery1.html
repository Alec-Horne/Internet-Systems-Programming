Photography Gallery

This gallery demonstrates the use of a responsive image gallery (images move depending on screen size) and also various image filters in CSS3. If you resize the window, the images will change location to accommodate the new screen size. The filter property does not work in some browsers such as Internet Explorer, Edge 12, and Safari 5.1 and earlier.
